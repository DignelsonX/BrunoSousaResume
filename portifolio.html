    SecurePass

    About this app

    SecurePass is a safety companion app designed for contractors working in hazardous environments. Once signed in, the app automatically detects when the user enters a designated hazard zone using geofencing technology and begins real-time location tracking. In case of an emergency, users can quickly request help by performing a long press on the emergency button (previously triggered by shaking the phone).
